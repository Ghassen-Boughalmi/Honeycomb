Compliance monitoring management

Supports the Compliance Monitoring Manager (CMM) in ensuring that all activities of the organisation are monitored for compliance with the applicable regulatory requirements and standards. As well as any additional requirements as established by the organisation and are being carried out properly under the supervision of the relevant head of the respective functional area
Supports the CMM in ensuring that the Compliance Monitoring Programme is properly implemented, maintained, continually reviewed and improved

Monitors the implementation and effectiveness of preventive and corrective action through the followup process

Promotes corporate culture for safety and compliance
Maintains records and documents of the Management System
Continuously monitors changes in legal framework in coordination with CMM
Liaison with regulatory authorities, original equipment manufacturers and other operationally relevant external organisations, as requested by CMM
